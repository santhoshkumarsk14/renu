Discover Our NDT Expertise

Ultrasonic Testing (UT)

Precision testing for detecting internal flaws and measuring material thickness, ensuring structural integrity.

Radiographic Testing (RT)

Advanced imaging to identify hidden defects in welds, castings, and pipelines with permanent records.

Magnetic Particle Testing (MT)

Quick and reliable detection of surface and near-surface defects in ferromagnetic materials.

Phased Array Ultrasonic Testing (PAUT)

Dynamic beam steering technology for enhanced weld inspections and plant monitoring.

Liquid Penetrant Testing (PT)

Effective surface flaw detection using capillary action for smooth, non-porous materials.

Long Range Ultrasonic Testing (LRUT)

Efficient inspection of pipelines and inaccessible areas, covering up to 150 meters in a single scan.
